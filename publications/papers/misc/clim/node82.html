Standard Windowing

The standard windowing contract supports multiple overlapping children in a windowing relationship that can dynamically be adopted and disowned. The children are ordered in top to bottom order. All the sheet protocols documented in Chapter 8 are supported.

Standard windowing allows its children's region and transformations to be of any region or transformation type respectively. Programmers will generally use rectangles and rectilinear transformations. More complex region or transformation types are not necessarily supported by all CLIM implementation on all display servers.
