Painting a gray or colored wash over a display.

Specify a translucent design as the ink, such as :ink (compose-in +black+ (make-opacity 0.25)), :ink (compose-in +red+ (make-opacity 0.1)), or :ink (compose-in +foreground-ink+ (make-opacity 0.75)). The last example can be abbreviated as :ink (make-opacity 0.75). On a non-color, non-grayscale display this will probably turn into a stipple.
