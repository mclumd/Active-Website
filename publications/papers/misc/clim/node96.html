Establishing Local Coordinate Systems

with-local-coordinates (medium &optional x y) &body body

Binds the dynamic environment to establish a local coordinate system on the medium designated by medium with the origin of the new coordinate system at the position 6#6. The ``directionality'' of the coordinate system is otherwise unchanged. x and y are real numbers, and both default to 0.

The medium argument is not evaluated, and must be a symbol that is bound to a sheet or medium. If medium is t, *standard-output* is used. body may have zero or more declarations as its first forms.

with-first-quadrant-coordinates (medium &optional x y) &body body

Binds the dynamic environment to establish a local coordinate system on the medium designated by medium with the positive 1#1 axis extending to the right and the positive 2#2 axis extending upward, with the origin of the new coordinate system at the position 6#6. x and y are real numbers, and both default to 0.

The medium argument is not evaluated, and must be a symbol that is bound to a sheet or medium. If medium is t, *standard-output* is used. body may have zero or more declarations as its first forms.
