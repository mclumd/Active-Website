Other Region Types

The other types of regions are points, polylines, polygons, elliptical arcs, and ellipses. All of these region types are closed under affine transformations.

SWM A reasonable way to address the perceived ``bloating'' caused by all of the following region types is to specify that CLIM must not internally use any region types except for points, rectangles, and region sets composed of rectangular regions. This allows CLIM implementations to omit support for the other region types from the ``core'' of CLIM, and include that support as a layered module.
