Changes from CLIM 1.0

This appendix lists the incompatible changes from CLIM 1.0 (and CLIM 0.9 for the Silica-related API), and the rationale for those changes. They are listed on a chapter-by-chapter basis.

When the items say that a compatibility stub will be provided, this does not mean that this compatibility needs to be part of CLIM itself. It could be provided by a small compatibility package, or by some sort of conversion utility.
