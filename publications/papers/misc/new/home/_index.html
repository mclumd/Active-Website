Active Logic Research Group

Welcome to the Active Logic home in the Computer Science Department at the University of Maryland at College Park.

Active logics are a family of inference engines that incorporate a history of their reasoning as they run. This characteristic makes Active Logic systems more flexible than traditional AI systems and therefore more suitable for commonsense reasoning.

Commonsense reasoning using active logics is a major theme in our research and we have been performing a number of experiments in that area.
