Tuesday, Nov. 21. 1995

TIME: 3:30 P.M. - 4:30 P.M.

PLACE: Room 3258 AVW Bldg. University of Maryland at College Park

SPEAKER: Dr. Vladimir Lifschitz

Gottesman Family Centennial Professor, Dept. of CS. Univ. of Texas at Austin

TITLE: ``What is Prolog?''

ABSTRACT:

The work of Prolog can be viewed as the process of proof search for a calculus associated with the given logic program. This interpretation of Prolog makes it easier to prove that it is sound with respect to the declarative semantics of logic programming.
