WEEKLY SEMINAR ON LOGIC AND AI

Tuesday, October 15, 1996

TIME: 3:30 P.M.

PLACE: Room 2120 AVW Bldg.

University of Maryland at College Park

SPEAKER:

Don Perlis
University of Maryland at College Park

TITLE: ``The Proper Role of Semantics in Modeling Common Sense''

ABSTRACT:

There is a mismatch between semantics as usually construed in formal AI, and rational behavior. I will describe several points of such mismatch, and then what I regard as a more appropriate role for semantics.

Everyone is welcome to attend.
