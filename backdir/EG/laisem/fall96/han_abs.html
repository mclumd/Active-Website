TITLE: ``Relation Query Optimization in Connection Hypergraphs''

ABSTRACT:

We use connection hypergraphs (CHGs) to optimize relational queries. Many widely used query optimization techniques can be easily simulated in CHGs. The search space for query execution plans (QEPs) may be organized into four dimensions. Then, an exhaustive search (System R) may be used to find the best QEP.

With the introduction of partial evaluations, we optimize nested queries and views, and perform sideways information passing strategies (SIPS) in CHGs. We propose the maximum SIPS strategy with the help of the CHG representation, which outperforms Kim's transformations and magic sets methods for relational queries. This approach can be generalized to deductive databases in the future.
