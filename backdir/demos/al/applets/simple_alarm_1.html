Simple alarm example

Description:

Whenever it is time 10, we assert lunch.

View the history applet
Alma specification

Run:

  • At step 1, Alma contains the description of the domain and the fact that the time is 1.
  • Nothing happens until now(10). At this point the antecedent of the formula is satisfied and
  • At step 11, lunch is asserted.

Note:

lunch is asserted one step later than we intended because of the time to reason about this. We could, of course, have taken that into account in the specification formula. More interesting though would be to have Alma realize itself that it has to start the reasoning earlier
