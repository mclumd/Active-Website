Settable alarm II example

Description:

The objective is similar to that of settable alarm I, but we specify it differently. This time we say: if lunch is at T, then add a formula that will assert lunch whenever it is T. THe difference here is that the now has a constant argument so that we do not instantiate several copies of the formula as in settable alarm I.

View the history applet
Alma specification

Run:

  • Initially nothing happens since the antecedent of the formula is lunch_at(X) and Alma does not know that.
  • At step 4 we assert that lunch is at 12.
  • This results, at step 6, in an assertion that whenever it is 12, then we should assert that it is lunch time.
  • Nothing happens until time 12.
  • At time 13, lunch appears in the KB.

Note:

Compare with Settable alarm I.
