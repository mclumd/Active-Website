The Nixon diamond

Description:

Republicans are usually not pacifists, quakers are usually pacfists, nixon is both quaker and republican. Is he a pacifist?

View the history applet
Alma specification

Default and contradiction handlers.
Prolog helper.

Run:

Note:

  • At step 1, all the information is present. We know that Nixon is a republican quaker and the defaults that republicans are not pacifists and quakers are.
  • At step 2, Alma attempts to apply both the defaults.
  • At step 3, Alma does not see any objection to applying them.
  • At step 4, Alma gets both that Nixon is pacifist and not.
  • This is noted as a contradiction at step 5 and Alma distrusts the contradictands.
  • Both the contradictands are found to depend on defaults at step 7.
  • These defaults are not related, so there is nothing much Alma can do now. Alma has no opinion as to whether Nixon is a pacifist or not.
