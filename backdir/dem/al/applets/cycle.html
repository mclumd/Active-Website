Cycle example

Description:

In this example, we imsgine turning a device on and off at regular intervals. We might want it to be ON at 10, stay on for 10 steps then be OFF for 10 steps and repeat that cycle. The start time and period of the cycling are specified using 
 cycle(Start, Period).

View the history applet
Alma specification

Run:

  • At step 3 we add cycle(5, 5).
  • At step 5, this triggers the inferences so that
  • Alma prepares to turn OFF at step 10, see 
     recycle(10, 5)
  • At step 8, ON is asserted and it stays on.
  • At step 10, Alma start reasoning about turning OFF.
  • At step 11, this sets up to turn back on at 15
  • At step 12 ON is deleted and OFF is added.
  • The cycle then repeats.

Note:

Although we specify that the cycle start at 5 and have period 5, this is not exactly what happens due to the reasoning time.
