Active Logic
Active Logic is a formalism that has been developed with real-world applications and their challenges in mind. Motivating its design is the thought that one of the factors that supports the flexibility of human reasoning is that it takes place step-wise, in time. Active Logic is one of a family on inference engines (step-logics) that explicitly reason in time, and incorporate a history of their reasoning as they run. This characteristic makes Active Logic systems more flexible than traditional AI systems and therefore more suitable for commonsense, real-world reasoning.
