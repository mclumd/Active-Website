Pointers to Presentations and Other Items of Interest on Metacognition

 

Unless otherwise noted, presentations are an hour at noon in conference room 3258 of A. V. Williams on the campus of the University of Maryland, College Park. The seminars are on Mondays usually each month. Brown bag lunches are encouraged.
